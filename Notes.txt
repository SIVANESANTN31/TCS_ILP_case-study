✅ 👤 Member 1 – Introduction
Slide 1 – Title Slide

Topic: How to Practice Integrity in the Workplace
This presentation explains how employees can act honestly, responsibly, and ethically in daily work.
Focus on real-life examples and practical workplace situations.


Slide 2 – What is Integrity?
Definition:
Integrity means doing the right thing even when nobody is watching.
Detailed Explanation:

It is about being honest in both actions and communication
It means following moral and ethical principles in every situation
Your behavior should remain consistent whether supervised or not

Key Values:

Honesty → Always speak the truth
Responsibility → Accept your actions
Accountability → Take ownership of results
Respect → Treat everyone fairly

Example:
Finishing tasks sincerely even when your manager is not monitoring you.

✅ 👤 Member 2 – Importance
Slide 3 – Why is Integrity Important?
Key Points:

Builds trust between team members and managers
Creates a positive and healthy work environment
Improves communication and reduces conflicts
Encourages teamwork and collaboration

Explanation:

When employees are honest, teams perform better
Trust reduces stress and increases efficiency

Example:
Employees who give honest updates are trusted with important tasks.

Slide 4 – Why Integrity Matters to the Company
Impact on Organization:

Builds strong relationships with customers
Improves company reputation and brand value
Prevents fraud, errors, and legal issues
Increases long-term business growth

Deeper Insight:

One dishonest act can damage the company’s image
Honest behavior increases client loyalty

Example:
Correcting billing mistakes keeps customer trust strong.

✅ 👤 Member 3 – Practical Tips (Part 1)
Slide 5 – Tip 1: Be Honest
What it means:

Always tell the truth, even in difficult situations
Do not hide mistakes or provide false information

Why it matters:

Honesty builds credibility
People trust employees who are transparent

Real-Life Example:

You delete an important file
Inform your manager immediately and try to recover it

Key Message:
Honesty leads to long-term trust and strong relationships.

Slide 6 – Tip 2: Take Responsibility
What it involves:

Accept mistakes without blaming others
Focus on solving the problem

Steps to follow:

Admit the mistake
Inform the concerned person
Correct the issue
Learn from it

Example:
Sending wrong email → Accept it → Correct it → Apologize
Key Message:
Responsible behavior earns respect and trust.

✅ 👤 Member 4 – Practical Tips (Part 2)
Slide 7 – Tip 3: Be Punctual
Importance:

Shows discipline and respect for others
Improves productivity and reliability

Practices:

Arrive at work on time
Attend meetings without delay
Complete tasks within deadlines

Example:
An employee who consistently meets deadlines is trusted with major projects.

Slide 8 – Tip 4: Follow Company Rules
Why rules exist:

Ensure fairness and discipline
Protect company resources and data

Examples:

Avoid misuse of office equipment
Follow security and confidentiality policies

Example:
Refusing to share confidential customer data with outsiders
Key Message:
Following rules protects both you and the company.

✅ 👤 Member 5 – Practical Tips (Part 3)
Slide 9 – Tip 5: Give Credit to Others
What it means:

Acknowledge team efforts
Do not take all the credit

Why it matters:

Builds trust within the team
Encourages collaboration and teamwork

Example:
Instead of saying “I did everything”, say “Our team worked together”
Key Message:
Integrity includes fairness and appreciation.

Slide 10 – Tip 6: Avoid Cheating & Protect Data
Avoid unethical behavior:

Do not copy work
Avoid fake reporting
Do not plagiarize

Protect confidential information:

Customer data
Company documents
Passwords

Example:
Refusing to share sensitive data even with close friends
Key Message:
Integrity means doing your own work and protecting information.

✅ 👤 Member 6 – Conclusion
Slide 11 – Internship Scenario
Situation:
You are asked if a task is complete, but only 70% is done.
Wrong Approach:

Saying “It is complete”

Right Approach:

Giving honest progress update
Explaining plan to complete remaining work

Result:

Builds trust with manager
Shows professionalism and honesty


Slide 12 – Benefits and Summary
Benefits of Integrity:

Builds trust and credibility
Improves career growth opportunities
Strengthens teamwork
Increases leadership potential

Summary:

Be honest in all situations
Take responsibility for actions
Respect rules and people
Work sincerely and ethically

Final Message:
Integrity is not just a value—it is a habit that builds a successful career and a strong organization.
